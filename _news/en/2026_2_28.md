Our work that experimentally demonstrated a spin-cat code and its single-qubit logical operations has been posted on [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22883). Joint work with Toshi Kusano, Kosuke Shibata, Chih-Han Yeh, Keito Saito, Yuma Nakamura, Rei Yokoyama, Takumi Kashimoto, Tetsushi Takano, Yosuke Takasu, and Yoshiro Takahashi.
