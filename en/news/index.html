2025 Apr Our work (by Ryuji) on quantum thermodynamics has been published in Physical Review Letters. We showed that “Gibbs-preserving operations”—one of the standard operations frequently used in anslysis—may require infinite cost to implement, encouraging us to reconsider physical justification of its use. Joint work with Hiroyasu Tajima at Kyushu University.
2025 Apr Posted a preprint (by Kaito and Ryuji) on quantum thermodynamics. We introduced a “universal work extractor”, which is designed independently of the input state but still extracts the optimal work from every input state given in the iid form.
